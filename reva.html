REVA is the information fair for people with disabilities, older people, carers, service providers and other professionals. A question about services? Looking for the right tool? You are sure to find the solution at the REVA information fair! You will meet many exhibitors and can participate in lectures and workshops. Everyone is welcome at REVA: people with and without disabilities, young and old, professional carers, informal carers... And so for me, this was the ideal opportunity to meet users. Because how can you be a website builder if you haven't met the users yourself and thus haven't tailored your product to their needs? So the REVA fair was definitely a cornerstone in building this website.
