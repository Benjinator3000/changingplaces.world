My name is Benjamin Mercy and I am a land surveying student at HOGent on a mission. This page has been built as a project within my field of study, choosing this particular subject because travelling is a big passion of mine. For me as a solo traveler accessibility is a basic amenity. How often does it happen that you go on a road trip or arrive in a new city where you know no one and nothing and you quickly want to freshen up or go to the toilet? I think this has happened to everyone at least once. For people with a disability this is even less evident, they need more space etc. Changing places did bring the solution to this problem as they sometimes have a shower to refresh yourself, a changing table, sink and toilet. And just like public disabled toilets these places are free to access. So like I said I‚Äôm on a mission to make traveling in Europe more accessible for everyone!
